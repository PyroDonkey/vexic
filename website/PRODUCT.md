# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Engineers building AI agent products, internal automation, or research systems — the people who decide what memory infrastructure their agents run on. They land on vexic.dev from a repo link, a comparison search ("mem0 vs zep alternatives"), or word of mouth. They are skeptical by default, allergic to hype, and evaluate by reading how the thing actually works. Their job to be done: decide whether Vexic is credible enough to join the hosted waitlist and keep on their radar.

## Product Purpose

Vexic gives long-running AI agents a memory they can trust: cleaned transcript history as source of truth, staged memories promoted with provenance, rebuildable indexes, auditable and reversible recall. Local-first Python/SQLite core today; hosted MCP/API and Console control-plane in progress. The website's success metric is qualified hosted-waitlist signups — email capture from engineers who understood the pitch, not drive-by traffic.

## Brand Personality

Rigorous, precise, engineering-first. Confidence through correctness: provenance, audit trails, conformance tests — shown, not claimed. The tone of a well-run infrastructure company (Railway, Vercel, Neon), not an AI startup. Emotional goal: "these people take memory correctness as seriously as I do."

References (specific traits to borrow):
- **Railway** — infra-serious dark surface; the product's actual mechanics rendered as the visual centerpiece.
- **Vercel** — typographic restraint and hierarchy; whitespace doing the work; zero decorative noise.
- **Neon** — deep-dark theme with one committed brand hue carrying identity; technical diagrams as first-class content.
- **Superset** — developer-tool directness; terminal/code-native presentation.

## Anti-references

- **mem0.ai and getzep.com (direct competitors)** — differentiate hard. Avoid the generic AI-SaaS look they occupy: light/pastel gradient washes, abstract AI illustration, hype adjectives ("supercharge", "10x"), hero-metric stat rows.
- Generic AI-startup grammar: sparkles/brain iconography, purple-to-pink gradients, mascots, "magic" framing.
- Landing-page scaffolding tells: identical icon-card grids, uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section, numbered section markers as decoration.

## Design Principles

1. **Show the mechanism, don't assert the benefit.** Provenance chains, staged→promoted memory flow, real API/config snippets — the pipeline itself is the hero, the way Railway shows deploys.
2. **Credibility through precision.** Exact numbers, real commands, honest caveats (hosted is waitlist-gated, local works today). Never round up capability.
3. **Infra register, not AI register.** Vexic sits with databases and deploy platforms, not chatbot tooling. Every visual choice should survive "would a Postgres user trust this?"
4. **One committed hue.** The emerald identity carries the brand; everything else is disciplined near-black neutrals. No palette sprawl.
5. **Restraint is the differentiator.** Competitors shout; Vexic states. Density of substance over density of decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: ≥4.5:1 body-text contrast (watch muted-foreground on dark surfaces), ≥3:1 large text, full keyboard navigability, visible focus states. Every animation ships a `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative (crossfade or instant). Color never sole information carrier.
